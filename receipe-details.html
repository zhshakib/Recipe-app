Cashew Nut Salad

Author: Coini | Likes: 999 | Published: May 8, 2025

Description

Enjoy this delicious and easy Cashew Nut Salad recipe! This salad is perfect for a light lunch or a side dish. It's packed with fresh flavors and the crunchiness of cashew nuts.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine salad greens, cashews, cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently. Serve immediately.
